Rmd 717cb23 LiaoQianWu 2023-08-22 Train MOFA models on latest omics data and write functions to automate procedures of MOFA training and analysis

Description: Train MOFA models with varied combinations of omics data. We got 12 Method Development Lung Cancer datasets: Two DIA Plasma/Tissue Proteomics, one DDA Plasma/Tissue Proteomics, one Targeted Plasma/Tissue Metabolomics, and one Untargeted Plasma/Tissue Metabolomics and Lipidomics. Some patient samples in some datasets were excluded or missing due to high level of missing values or patient dropout. Based on our previous finding that Plasma samples provide higher proportions of significant cancer recurrence-related features than Tissue samples, plus Plasma samples are easier to obtain in real-life clinical scenario, our strategy to train models are first using only Plasma omics data and then add Tissue omics data to see changes in model performance and interpretability.
