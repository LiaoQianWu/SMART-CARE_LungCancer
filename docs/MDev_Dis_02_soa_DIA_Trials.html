It may be that Condition accounts for major variance in data, so some associations with constructed SVs are inevitable. For Recurrence, it may be due to outliler samples observed in molecular signatures, especially of Normal samples (check Section Normal samples - UNADJUSTED). Those outlier samples are mostly from Recurrence patient groups, and SVA tries to estimate differences between outlier and the other samples (uninteresting variance), which makes those SVs associated with Recurrence.
