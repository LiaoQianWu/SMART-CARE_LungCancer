Rmd b608341 LiaoQianWu 2024-01-26 Train RF models and do feature selection

Description: Do feature selection with lasso logistic regression models trained on combined data of cohorts (Method Development and Discovery) to identify potential biomarkers for predicting lung cancer recurrence. Additional feature filtering on subdatasets is not conducted, so top important features selected by models are mostly highly missing. There is risk that imputation method (missForest) used in our workflow introducing biases and impeding true but weaker signals contributed by features more complete. On other hand, with brief literature research, those top important features with high level of missingness seem associated with lung adenocarcinoma. Moreover, imputed values seem sensible because they are usually of low abundance, which matches property of MS-based data that missingness is often MNAR. Next action will be using data imputed by our developing imputation method (msBayesImpute) to train models to do feature selection and checking result consistency, which may provides more confidence in our findings.
